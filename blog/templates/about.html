About Mountain of Metal

Mountain of Metal is an underground metal focused review platform dedicated to helping readers discover new music and stay up to date about the music we love. Although this is a metal focused platform we also review adjacent genres such as post-rock, shoegaze, ambient, and folk.

Mountain of Metal was founded by Joshua Landau, a lifelong metalhead and musician with a long history with music. At age 7 he learned how to play the guitar and shortly after started experimenting with writing and recording his own music. Joshua’s connection to metal is what has led him to write, record, produce, and now review music. With 18 years of studying and playing music, and 10 years of recording and producing music, his taste is trusted amongst metalheads and normies alike.

Favorite genres: Atmospheric black metal, Doom metal, Death metal

Some favorite artists: Ensiferum, Panopticon, Falls of Rauros, Agalloch, Yob, Warbringer, Black Dahlia Murder, Suffocation

Early Musical Inspirations: Metallica, Judas Priest, Iron Maiden, Dio, Black Sabbath, Children of Bodom
